  • ENABLE [read/write]: enable
    Enable Single Pulse Generation.
  • RESERVED [read/write]: Not used
    Not used.
  • STOP [write-only]: Stop pulse
    Stops and resets current pulse on writing 1.
  • SOFTTRIGGER [write-only]: Soft trigger
    Generates a trigger on writing 1.
